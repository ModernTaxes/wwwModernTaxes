IRS Enrolled Agent

Enrolled Agents (EAs) are the only federally-licensed tax practitioners who may represent taxpayers before the IRS when it comes to collections, audits, and appeals. Under the Department of Treasury's Circular 230 regulations, EAs are granted unlimited practice rights to represent taxpayers before the IRS and are authorized to advise, represent, and prepare tax returns. As the Department of Treasury regulates all Enrolled Agents, this license is recognized in all 50 states and territories.
